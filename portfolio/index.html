Who am I.

My name is Dídac Pérez Escrich and I am 23-year-old from Barcelona. After getting a B. Sc. in Physics (and a B.Sc. in Chemistry as well) I have decided to make a transition in my career to focus completely on the digital world. I believe that being present digitally is a matter of utmost importance nowadays - and it will be even greater in the near future. That is one of the reasons why I am deciding to invest in becoming a software developer.

I also find my studies in the field of physics, or in science, a great complement to perform this task. Throughout the years I have gathered a rational point-of-view, a regime of critical thinking and the ability and problem-solving capabilities. Research, state of the art, being able to quickly adapt to new metholodogies also played an important part in my career at university.

Besides, I focused my thesis in the theoretical spectrum, thus being forced to perform multiple simulations in order to gather an important amount of data which I had, then, to analyse. This has made me familiar with several programing languages such as C, C++, Python; with some of the most famous data-analysis and data-visualization software such as MatLab and Origin; and with Linux distributions.
